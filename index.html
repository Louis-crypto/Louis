Annual Drama Festivals

The Kenya Schools and Colleges Drama Festival is student competitions in different kind of scripts it was first held in 1959, and it is held annually from January to April. It involves different stages in which schools advance from i.e from local stages to county stages to regional and then the final stage is the nationals. Its covers al levels of education in kenya from primary, secondary and college, which is a total of approximately five million students which makes it the largest eduacation event in Africa. The first college drama festivals were held in 1975 while the first primary school festivals were held in 1984. In the recent years the traditional plays have been expanded to include; dramatised verse, cultural traditional dance, african story telling narratives, stand-up comedy, modern dance and film.
